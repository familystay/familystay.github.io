About FamilyStay

Travelling with kids should be fun and comfortable.

We like to travel a lot with our kids, but traditional hotel rooms are not the best option. My goal is to provide family-friendly accomodation, just like we would want to have ourselves when we travel.

  • Enough space

    We know that you need room with family. Apartments have kitchen facilities so you can cook yourself, and you can still enjoy your evening time, when kids sleep in another room.

  • Like a local

    You will stay next to local families in residential areas close to playgrounds and amenities, but it's still centrally located. We also give you local hints, so you can feel like home right from the start.

  • All you need

    Our apartments are equipped with toys, utensils and other stuff your kids need, so you don't have to bring that extra luggage when you travel.
